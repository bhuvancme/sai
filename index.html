ABOUT SCIENCE DAY CARNIVAL

A Science Day Carnival is an event that celebrates and promotes science through interactive exhibits, demonstrations, and activities. It often aims to make science engaging and accessible to people of all ages, fostering a love for learning and discovery. Participants may explore various scientific fields through hands-on experiments, workshops, and educational displays.

A science exhibition is an organized display of scientific projects, experiments, and innovations. It provides a platform for students, researchers, and enthusiasts to showcase their work and share scientific knowledge with the public. Science exhibitions often cover a diverse range of topics, including physics, chemistry, biology, technology, and environmental science. These events aim to stimulate curiosity, promote scientific awareness, and inspire interest in the wonders of science.
